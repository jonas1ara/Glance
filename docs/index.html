About Glance

"It's honestly embarrassing that Chrome accidentally became the best PDF viewer while Adobe spent decades making Acrobat slower and more bloated."

— The problem Glance solves: Windows needed a simple PDF viewer

Just as GNOME Evince brought lightweight, elegant PDF viewing to Linux with GTK, Glance brings that same philosophy to Windows with Fluent Design.

We believe in:

  • Simplicity over bloat: Do one thing, and do it well.
  • Native aesthetics: Respect the platform you're building for.
  • Open-source foundation: Transparent, auditable, community-driven.
  • Accessibility: Free for developers and open-source contributors.
  • Fair pricing: Reasonable cost for pre-packaged convenience.
  • No bloat, no ads: We're committed to delivering a minimal, focused PDF viewer.
  • No forced AI: We won't add gimmicky AI integrations just for hype.

Our commitment: You wanted a PDF viewer. That's what we're giving you. No ads. No tracking. No unnecessary AI features or gimmicks. Just a fast, beautiful way to read PDFs on Windows.

Glance is distributed as Open Source to the developer community, with optional paid packaging for non-technical users who prefer convenience over compilation.

Backend Layer

Rust

Native performance for PDF rendering, validation, and persistent storage.

FFI Bridge
